# Hetman's Shpundra

National Ukrainina Dish.

## Ingredients

- pork belly
- flour
- garlic
- onions
- beetroot
- beetroot juice (kwas)
- salt
- sugar
- peper

## Steps

1. Fry garlic - add to pot
2. Onion + peper + salt + sugar - fry - add to pot
3. Pork bell cover with four + satl + pepper - fry
4. Slice beetroot add to pot
5. Add beetroot juice

Cook for 1 hour.

Enjoy.
